# Quick Records

Welcome to Quick Records, your most secure platform to manage your shop inventory and generate your invoices the easy way.

## Live Demo

You can check out the live demo of Quick Records at [https://qck-rcd.vercel.app/](https://qck-rcd.vercel.app/).

## Features

- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration system to protect your data.
- **Inventory Management**: Easily add, edit, and delete products in your inventory.
- **Invoice Generation**: Create and manage invoices for your customers with just a few clicks.
- **Data Security**: Your data is encrypted and stored securely to ensure your privacy.

## Technologies Used
- **Frontend**: Next.js, Tailwind CSS
- **Database**: PostgreSQL(via Prisma ORM)
- **Authentication**: NextAuth, JWT (JSON Web Tokens)
